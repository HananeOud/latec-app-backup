1. SCOPE

This specification defines the coating and paint requirements for the full
renovation of Riverside Commercial Complex (12 buildings, mixed-use: retail,
office, residential). The selected supplier SHALL demonstrate compliance with
every requirement listed below. Non-compliance on any critical requirement
is grounds for disqualification from the bid.

================================================================================

2. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

REQ-GEN-001: All coatings supplied MUST be from a single manufacturer to ensure
system compatibility across primer, base coat, and topcoat layers.

REQ-GEN-002: The supplier SHALL provide complete Safety Data Sheets (SDS) and
Technical Data Sheets (TDS) for every product proposed.

REQ-GEN-003: All products MUST be available for delivery within 10 business days
of order confirmation for quantities up to 800 gallons.

REQ-GEN-004: The supplier SHALL offer on-site technical consultation and
application training for our painting crews prior to project start.

REQ-GEN-005: All products MUST be compliant with current EPA VOC regulations
for architectural coatings. Maximum VOC content: 50 g/L for interior flat,
100 g/L for exterior coatings.

REQ-GEN-006: The supplier SHALL provide a dedicated account manager for the
duration of the project (estimated 8 months).

================================================================================

3. EXTERIOR COATING REQUIREMENTS

REQ-EXT-001: Exterior walls REQUIRE a weather-resistant topcoat rated for a
minimum of 10 years of UV and rain protection without significant fading or
chalking.

REQ-EXT-002: The exterior coating system MUST include a rust-inhibiting primer
for all metal substrates (railings, structural steel, door frames).

REQ-EXT-003: The rust protection primer MUST provide a minimum of 8 years of
corrosion resistance on ferrous metals.

REQ-EXT-004: Exterior coatings MUST be applicable at temperatures between
50°F (10°C) and 95°F (35°C) with relative humidity up to 85%.

REQ-EXT-005: The exterior topcoat SHALL achieve a minimum dry film thickness
of 3.0 mils per coat.

REQ-EXT-006: All exterior coatings MUST resist mildew and algae growth for a
minimum of 5 years.

REQ-EXT-007: Exterior metal coatings MUST be compatible with hot-dip galvanized
steel surfaces without requiring a separate bonding agent.

================================================================================

4. INTERIOR COATING REQUIREMENTS

REQ-INT-001: Interior common areas (lobbies, hallways, stairwells) REQUIRE a
semi-gloss finish with a sheen level between 35-70 units at 60°.

REQ-INT-002: The interior semi-gloss coating MUST be water-based (latex/acrylic)
for low odor during application in occupied spaces.

REQ-INT-003: Interior coatings MUST be washable and scrubbable — minimum
Class 1 scrub resistance per ASTM D2486.

REQ-INT-004: Interior coatings for residential units SHALL achieve full cure
within 14 days and be safe for occupancy within 48 hours of final coat.

REQ-INT-005: The supplier MUST offer a tintable base system with a minimum
of 1,500 color options for interior residential units.

REQ-INT-006: Interior coatings MUST meet GREENGUARD Gold certification or
equivalent for low chemical emissions.

REQ-INT-007: Coverage rate for interior coatings SHALL be a minimum of
350 sq ft per gallon on smooth drywall surfaces.

================================================================================

5. PRIMER & SURFACE PREPARATION

REQ-PRM-001: The supplier SHALL provide a universal primer compatible with
drywall, wood, previously painted surfaces, and metal substrates.

REQ-PRM-002: The primer MUST be stain-blocking — capable of covering water
stains, smoke damage, and tannin bleed from wood.

REQ-PRM-003: Primer dry-to-recoat time SHALL NOT exceed 2 hours under standard
conditions (72°F / 50% RH).

REQ-PRM-004: The primer MUST be sandable after 1 hour for surface preparation
between coats.

REQ-PRM-005: The primer system MUST provide adhesion to glossy or previously
coated surfaces without requiring mechanical sanding.

================================================================================

6. SUSTAINABILITY & ENVIRONMENTAL

REQ-ENV-001: The supplier MUST demonstrate that at least 80% of products in the
proposed system use bio-based or recycled content materials.

REQ-ENV-002: All packaging MUST be recyclable. The supplier SHALL offer a
container return/recycling program.

REQ-ENV-003: Products MUST be manufactured using renewable energy sources
(minimum 50% of production facility energy).

REQ-ENV-004: The supplier SHALL provide Environmental Product Declarations (EPD)
or equivalent lifecycle documentation.

REQ-ENV-005: All coatings MUST be zero-APE (alkylphenol ethoxylate free) and
formaldehyde-free.

================================================================================

7. COMMERCIAL & LOGISTICS

REQ-COM-001: The supplier MUST offer Net 30 payment terms for approved
commercial accounts.

REQ-COM-002: Pricing MUST include volume discount tiers for orders exceeding
500 gallons per month.

REQ-COM-003: The supplier SHALL maintain a regional warehouse or distribution
point within 200 miles of the project site (Portland, OR metro area).

REQ-COM-004: The supplier MUST provide batch traceability — each delivery SHALL
include lot numbers and manufacturing dates.

REQ-COM-005: The supplier SHALL guarantee color consistency across batches
(Delta E < 1.0 between production lots).

================================================================================

8. COMPLIANCE VERIFICATION

The supplier SHALL respond to this specification with a Compliance Matrix
indicating, for each requirement:

    - Status: COMPLIANT / PARTIAL / NON-COMPLIANT
    - Evidence: Product name, data sheet reference, or test report
    - Notes: Any deviations or alternative proposals

Non-response to any requirement will be treated as NON-COMPLIANT.
